# Passenger Car Seatbelt - Always Required

## Question
Does a passenger car driver have to wear a seatbelt if the trip is short?

## Options
- **A) Yes, always** ✅
- **B) No, if driving in urban roads**
- **C) No, if performing loading and unloading operations at a short distance** ❌ (my answer)

## Correct Answer
A — Yes, always.

## Why I Got It Wrong
把 "passenger car" 理解成"拉乘客的车"（出租车），实际上 passenger car = turismo = 私家车/小客车。私家车司机不属于任何免系安全带的特例。

## Key Rule
免系安全带的特例（教材 p.192）：
- 市内市外：倒车/停车时；严重医疗证明/残疾
- 仅限城区：出租车司机（工作中）；短距离频繁装卸的送货员；驾校陪同人员；紧急服务车辆

"passenger car driver" 不在任何特例中 → always。

## Key Vocabulary
- **passenger car** = turismo = 私家车/小客车（不是"载客车"）
- taxi driver = 出租车司机（有城区免系特例）
- loading and unloading = 装卸货（送货员才有此特例）
