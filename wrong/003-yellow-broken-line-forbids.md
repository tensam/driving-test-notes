# Yellow Broken Line

## Question
What does the yellow broken line forbid?

## Options
- A) Both. ❌ (my answer)
- B) To stop.
- **C) To park.** ✅

## Correct Answer
**C) To park.**

## Explanation
- **Yellow broken line** = No parking (but stopping is allowed)
- **Yellow continuous line** = No stopping AND no parking (both forbidden)

The broken/dashed yellow line only prohibits parking. You CAN stop temporarily beside it.

## Key Rule
| Line Type | Parking | Stopping |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Yellow broken (dashed) | ❌ Forbidden | ✅ Allowed |
| Yellow continuous (solid) | ❌ Forbidden | ❌ Forbidden |
