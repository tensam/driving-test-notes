# Car Fire Extinguisher - Not Mandatory

## Question
Among the mandatory equipment that a car must carry, is there a fire extinguisher?

## Options
- **A) No** ✅
- B) Yes, but only if it has an authorised capacity of more than five litres
- **C) Yes, but only when driving on roads outside populated areas** ❌ (my answer)

## Correct Answer
A — No.

## Why I Got It Wrong
以为灭火器是私家车的强制装备之一，混淆了私家车和重型车辆的装备要求。

## Key Rule
私家车（turismo）强制携带装备：
- 两个三角警示牌（warning triangles）
- 反光马甲（reflective vest）
- 备胎或修补工具

灭火器是**货车、大巴、重型车辆**的强制装备，私家车不要求。

## Key Vocabulary
- fire extinguisher = extintor = 灭火器
- mandatory equipment = equipamiento obligatorio = 强制装备
- warning triangle = triángulo de emergencia = 三角警示牌
