# Double Parking - Absolutely Prohibited

## Question
Is double parking prohibited?

## Options
- **A) Yes** ✅
- **B) No, but the driver must not leave the vehicle** ❌ (my answer)
- **C) No, only stopping next to another vehicle is prohibited**

## Correct Answer
A — Yes, absolutely prohibited.

## Why I Got It Wrong
把临停（stopping）的"人不离车"规则错误套用到 double parking。Double parking 没有任何例外，连临停都不允许。

## Key Rule
Double parking（并排停在已停车辆旁边）= 绝对禁止，无例外。

## Key Vocabulary
- double parking = doble fila = 并排停车/双排停车
- stopping = 临停（短暂停车，人不离车）
- parking = 长停（停车离车）
