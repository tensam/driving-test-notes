# Road Works Speed Sign - Applies to All Vehicles

## Question
What does the sign indicate? (circular speed limit sign in road works zone)

## Options
- **A) The maximum speed at which I must drive during a stretch of road works** ✅
- **B) The maximum speed at which I can drive on this road**
- **C) The maximum speed for the road working vehicles** ❌ (my answer)

## Correct Answer
A — The maximum speed at which I must drive during a stretch of road works.

## Why I Got It Wrong
把施工路段的限速牌理解为"只限施工车辆"。实际上限速牌适用于所有经过施工路段的车辆，"road works"是路段条件描述，不是限速对象。

A vs B 的区别：A 说的是临时限速（施工路段有效），B 说的是这条路的永久限速。施工区限速是临时性质的。

## Key Rule
施工路段圆形限速标志 = 该路段临时最高速度，适用于所有通过的车辆。

## Key Vocabulary
- road works = obras = 施工路段
- stretch of road works = 施工路段区间
- road working vehicles = 施工车辆（不是限速对象）
