# Shoulder Parking on Interurban Road - Legal

## Question
Is the vehicle observed on the right side of the photograph correctly parked?

## Options
- **A) Yes, as long as the parking lasts less than two minutes**
- **B) Yes, because it is outside the drivable area of the shoulder** ✅
- **C) No, because it obstructs** ❌ (my answer)

## Correct Answer
B — Yes, because it is outside the drivable area of the shoulder.

## Why I Got It Wrong
把城外公路的路肩（shoulder）误认为人行道（pavement）。城外公路没有人行道，白线外是路肩。停在路肩可行车区域外侧 = 合法停车。

## Key Rule
| 场景 | 白线外是什么 | 能停车吗 |
|------|-----------|---------|
| 城外公路 | 路肩（shoulder） | ✅ 停在可行车区域外侧 |
| 城区道路 | 人行道（pavement） | ❌ 绝对禁止 |

## Key Vocabulary
- shoulder = arcén = 路肩
- drivable area = 可行车区域
- interurban road = 城外公路
- pavement = acera = 人行道
