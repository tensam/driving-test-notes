# Additional Lane - Lighting Requirement

## Question
On this roadway with an additional lane, which vehicles are driving correctly?

## Options
- **A) Only the vehicle driving in the central lane** ✅
- **B) All three vehicles using the roadway are driving correctly**
- **C) The two vehicles driving in the same direction** ❌ (my answer)

## Correct Answer
A — Only the vehicle driving in the central lane.

## Why I Got It Wrong
看到"additional lane"就去研究车道定义和位置，忽略了题目问的是"哪辆车的行为正确"。实际考点是灯光：附加车道路段要求所有车辆必须开近光灯（白天也要），图中只有中间车道的车开了近光灯。

## Key Rule
教材 p.112 — Additional Lane:
- Speed: All 80 km/h max / 60 km/h min
- **Lighting: All short range, at least, day and night**（所有车辆必须至少开近光灯，白天晚上都要）
- Users: The authorized to go on the road can use it

附加车道 / 反方向车道 / 可逆车道 → 全部车辆必须开近光灯，白天也要。

## Key Vocabulary
- additional lane = carril adicional = 附加车道
- short range (headlights) = 近光灯
- dipped headlights = 近光灯
