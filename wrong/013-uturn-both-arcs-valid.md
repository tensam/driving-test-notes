# U-turn - Both Wide and Tight Arcs Are Valid

## Question
In this situation, which vehicle has completed the manoeuvre properly?
(Two vehicles making U-turns: vehicle 1 wide arc, vehicle 2 tight arc)

## Options
- **A) Both.** ✅
- B) 2. ❌ (my answer)
- C) 1.

## Correct Answer
**A) Both.**

## Why I Got It Wrong
把左转的"必须拐大弯"规则错误套用到掉头上。

## Key Rule
- **左转**：必须绕过交叉口中心（拐大弯），路径有严格规定
- **掉头（U-turn）**：只要安全完成，宽弧和窄弧都合法，没有路径限制

教材p135：U-turn要求选合适地点、尽量不阻碍、一次完成、不用倒车，但没有规定弧线大小。
