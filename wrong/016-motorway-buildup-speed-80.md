# Motorway Speed Limit in Built-up Areas = 80 km/h

## Question
What is the speed limit for cars on motorways in built-up areas where there are no signs?

## Options
- A) 120 km/h. ❌ (my answer)
- **B) 80 km/h.** ✅
- C) 100 km/h.

## Correct Answer
**B) 80 km/h.**

## Why I Got It Wrong
看到"motorway"就直接选120，忽略了题目中"**in built-up areas**"这个关键限定。

## Key Rule
| 道路类型 | 城区外 | **城区内（built-up）** |
|--------|------|----------|
| Motorway / Dual carriageway | 120 | **80** |
| Conventional road | 90 | 50 |

**高速进城 = 80，不是120。**

## Key Vocabulary
- **built-up area** = 城区（poblado）→ 看到这个词，速度要大幅降低
- **motorway** = autopista/autovía = 高速公路
