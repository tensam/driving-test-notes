# Battery Liquid = Distilled Water

## Question
What liquid must be added to a battery when required?

## Options
- A) Water and anti-freeze. ❌ (my answer)
- **B) Distilled water.** ✅

## Correct Answer
**B) Distilled water.**

## Why I Got It Wrong
混淆了电池补液和冷却系统补液。防冻液（anti-freeze）是加到散热器（radiator）冷却系统的，不是电池。

## Key Rule
| 部件 | 补充液体 |
|------|---------|
| **电池（battery）** | **蒸馏水（distilled water）** |
| 散热器（radiator） | 冷却液/防冻液（coolant/anti-freeze） |

电池需要纯净的蒸馏水，含矿物质的普通水会损坏电池。

## Key Vocabulary
- **distilled water** = agua destilada = 蒸馏水
- **anti-freeze** = anticongelante = 防冻液
- **battery** = batería = 电池
