# ABS - Keeps Steering Control, Not Always Shorter Stop

## Question
The anti-lock braking system, ABS,...

## Options
- **A) always reduces the braking distance** ❌ (my answer)
- **B) makes the vehicle keep the trajectory**
- **C) allows the driver to keep control on the steering system** ✅

## Correct Answer
C — allows the driver to keep control on the steering system.

## Why I Got It Wrong
以为 ABS 主要功能是缩短刹车距离。实际上 ABS 防止车轮锁死，核心功能是让驾驶员急刹时仍能控制方向盘。在砂石/雪地上 ABS 可能增加刹车距离，所以 "always" 是错的。

## Key Rule
| ABS 功能说法 | 对错 |
|------------|------|
| 缩短刹车距离 | ❌ 不一定，看路面 |
| 保持车辆轨迹 | ❌ 不会自动保持 |
| **让驾驶员保持转向控制** | **✅** |

## Key Vocabulary
- anti-lock braking system (ABS) = 防抱死制动系统
- braking distance = 刹车距离
- steering system = 转向系统
