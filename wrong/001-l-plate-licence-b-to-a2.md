# L-plate: Licence B to A2

## Question
If you have had licence B for more than one year and now you obtain licence A2, are you required to display the L-plate in your motorcycle?

## Options
- **A) No.** ✅
- B) Yes, in the front for one year.
- C) Yes, in the rear for one year. ❌ (my answer)

## Correct Answer
**A) No.**

## Explanation
If you already held licence B for more than one year, you are considered an experienced driver. The L-plate requirement only applies to novice drivers (those who have held their first licence for less than one year). Since you've had B for over a year, obtaining A2 does not restart the L-plate requirement.

## Key Rule
L-plate obligation is tied to being a **new driver** (first licence < 1 year), not to obtaining an additional licence category.
