# Personal Transporters - Alcohol Test Obligation

## Question
Are drivers of personal transporters required to take a test to detect the presence of alcohol?

## Options
- A) Only if they are of legal age.
- B) No, since these vehicles do not have a motor. ❌ (my answer)
- **C) Yes.** ✅

## Correct Answer
**C) Yes.**

## Why I Got It Wrong
1. 电动滑板车**有电机**，选项B的事实前提就错
2. 即使没有马达也要测——教材p224：ALL drivers of vehicles (including cyclists) 必须接受酒精测试

## Key Rule
酒精测试义务覆盖**所有上路车辆驾驶者**，无一例外：
- 汽车 ✅ | 摩托车 ✅ | 自行车 ✅ | 电动滑板车 ✅
