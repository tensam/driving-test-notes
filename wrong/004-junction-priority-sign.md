# Junction Priority Sign (Merge from Right)

## Question
What does this sign mean?

(Red triangle warning sign with an arrow merging from the right)

## Options
- **A) Junction with priority over vehicles merging from the right.** ✅
- B) Crossroads with right of way to the right. ❌ (my answer)
- C) Junction where you must give way.

## Correct Answer
**A) Junction with priority over vehicles merging from the right.**

## Why I Got It Wrong
没读懂 A 和 B 两个选项的区别。

## Key Vocabulary & Explanation

| English | 中文 | 含义 |
|---------|------|------|
| **priority over** | 优先于 | **你有优先权**，别人让你 |
| **merging from the right** | 从右侧汇入 | 右边有车汇入你的路 |
| **right of way to the right** | 右侧来车有优先权 | **右边的车优先**，你要让他 |

### A vs B 的核心区别
- **A: 你有优先权** → 右边汇入的车要让你
- **B: 右边的车有优先权** → 你要让右边的车

这是完全**相反**的意思！

## Sign Recognition
- 三角形警告标志 + 箭头从右侧汇入 → 警告你右边有车要汇入，但**你有优先权**
- 如果是你要让路，标志会是倒三角（yield）或写着 "give way"
