# Registration Certificate Renewal = 30 Days

## Question
A vehicle buyer must request a registration certificate or permit renewal within...

## Options
- A) Fifteen days of purchase.
- B) Ten days of purchase. ❌ (my answer)
- **C) Thirty days of purchase.** ✅

## Correct Answer
**C) Thirty days of purchase.**

## Why I Got It Wrong
纯记忆题，记错了数字。

## Key Rule
买车后办登记证/过户期限 = **30天**。
