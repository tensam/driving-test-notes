# Narrow Street - Cannot Occupy Pavement

## Question
You need to make a stop in a narrow street. Can you occupy part of the pavement with your vehicle?

## Options
- **A) No.** ✅
- B) Yes, but only when there is no sign prohibiting so. ❌ (my answer)
- C) Yes.

## Correct Answer
**A) No.**

## Why I Got It Wrong
错误套用了"没有禁止标志就可以"的逻辑。人行道是**默认禁止**区域，不需要任何标志。

## Key Rule
人行道（pavement/sidewalk）= 行人专属 → 车辆**任何情况**都不能占用：
- 不管街多窄 ❌
- 不管有没有禁止标志 ❌
- 不管只占一部分还是全部 ❌

教材p141 8.6B-5: "On the sidewalks, walkways and other areas intended for pedestrian crossing" → 禁止长停。
教材p141 8.6A-3: "In pedestrian crossings, level crossings and paths of cyclists" → 禁止短停和长停。
